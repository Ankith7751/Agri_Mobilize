PROPERTY

Real estate is considered real property that includes land and anything permanently attached to it or built on it, whether natural or man-made. There are five main categories of real estate which include residential, commercial, industrial, raw land, and special use. Investing in real estate includes purchasing a home, rental property, or land. Indirect investment in real estate can be made via REITs or through pooled real estate investment..

Transport

As the world seeks to develop public transport to reduce travel times, traffic, emissions, accidents, and bring other benefits, there is a massive barrier in its way: the financial aspect. The fare charged by the operators usually isn't enough to pay for the system's construction and operation costs, being necessary to be complemented with other revenue sources. One of the strategies that can help the implementation of transport infrastructure projects is real estate development.
