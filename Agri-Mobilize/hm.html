Collateral Evaluation

Collateral evaluation is a critical component of the our process,
and it involves assessing the value and quality of assets that
a borrower pledges as security for a loan. In the context of
agricultural equipment financing, collateral evaluation is particularly
relevant as the equipment itself often serves as the primary collateral.

Government Programs

Government programs in the context of agricultural equipment lending

These programs aim to provide financial assistance, incentives,

Support for Equipment Upgrades

Support for equipment upgrades from Agri-Mobilize involves
providing financial assistance or incentives for farmers to replace or
upgrade their existing agricultural machinery and equipment.
This support is designed to encourage the adoption of newer, more efficient,
and technologically advanced equipment.
